Privacy Policy

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the www.creditarget.com and Creditarget and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Creditarget include www.creditarget.com and Creditarget. The Creditarget website is a information and to repair credit site. By using the Creditarget website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

Creditarget may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name. If you purchase Creditarget's products and services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Creditarget. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Creditarget website.

Creditarget encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Creditarget so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Creditarget is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Creditarget website.

Use of your Personal Information

Creditarget collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

Creditarget may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Creditarget and its affiliates. Creditarget may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Creditarget does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

Creditarget may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is transferred to the third party. Creditarget may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Creditarget, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Creditarget may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within Creditarget, in order to determine what Creditarget services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Creditarget to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Creditarget will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Creditarget or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Creditarget; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Creditarget, or the public.

Security of your Personal Information

To secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure, Creditarget uses the following:

  • GeoTrust
  • Verisign

When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol.
