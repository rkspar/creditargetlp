Additional Services

These elite tools and high performance credit strategies maximize your credit score and financial well-being. Ask us about how these services can benefit you!

Credit Building

Building a good financial foundation is directly related to all your financial decisions. Credit Building acts as a personal assistant in helping pay utility, cell phone, and other bills in your name. In addition, we can recommend and assist in opening a secured checking and savings account, a loan with a cosigner, and sign you up as an authorized user on a trusted person’s account, which will demonstrate financial responsibility. Having a Creditarget professional sign you up with a secured card or loan is typically a great start to credit history because your security deposit is equal to your credit line. This type of card activity is then shared with the major credit bureaus. Creditarget can also calculate your debt-to-income ratio.

Expedited Service

Increased scrutiny of loan applications means that getting a mortgage can be a nail-biting experience. For some, there is that heart-stopping moment when the loan officer says “There is a problem with your credit”. What if that “problem” is a mistake and you don’t have the time to fix it? Creditarget’s Expedited Service is the most timely method for having data corrected in the credit report that will end up going directly to the lender that reported it. Sometimes data in question must be verified in 30 days, but Creditarget is able to go straight to the lender, the bureaus, and the banks to receive a response from the consumer reporting agency (CRA) within ten days.

Wish List

Create your very own Wish List of items and future purchases that will have a specific timetable showing exact progress you are making to achieving that goal based on credit and personal finances. This tool is an effective way to keep tabs on interests that you do not have immediate intent to purchase. Wish Lists have become more popular and relevant to the ecommerce experience as 60% of customers use mobile devices to research and order online. A Wish List will encourage engagement in credit building because you will know when you can pay for certain items.

Credit Coaching

Our experienced credit professionals are certified by the Credit Consultants Association and aim to deliver practical advice and insight through respectful, private one-on-one coaching sessions through phone and email. Credit Coaching is the most effective way to maintain a lifelong education in financial literacy through the wide range of strategies and tools. Our credit professionals help with basic finances, credit management, education finances, home ownership, investing, retirement, insurance protection, and small business credit lines.
